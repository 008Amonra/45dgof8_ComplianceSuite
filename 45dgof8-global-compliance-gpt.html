No. It provides general compliance guidance and helps you structure your thinking. For high-stakes or jurisdiction-specific decisions, involve qualified counsel.

What should I share when asking questions?

Share only what’s necessary. Avoid personal data, secrets, or customer identifiers. You can describe systems and flows in general terms.
