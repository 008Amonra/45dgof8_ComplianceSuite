45dgof8-pulse GDPR Overview

Verified by GDPR Nibbler – Metadata-only • No fund custody • Schrems II–safe

DPIA Template – 45dgof8-pulse

1. Processing Description
45dgof8-pulse is a coordination platform that facilitates metadata-based revenue sharing and payment link generation. It does not process, store, or transfer personal financial data or funds directly.

2. Purpose of Processing
To support automation and coordination of payments using metadata tags, including invoice generation, revenue splits, and transactional insights, without custodial involvement.

3. Lawfulness of Processing
The platform relies on user-initiated metadata and does not independently process personal data. No profiling or automated decision-making is used. Users retain full control over data sent to external payment systems.

4. Data Flow and Transfers
All data remains within the user’s chosen infrastructure. No data is routed through unsafe jurisdictions. The system is designed to comply with Schrems II implications by avoiding transatlantic transfers of personal data.

5. Risks and Mitigations
• Risk of metadata misuse - mitigated by transparent documentation.
• Legal misclassification - mitigated via clear disclaimers and no fund custody.
• Cross-border compliance - handled via DNS-Manager-GPT to enforce regional routing policies.

6. Contact & Responsibility
Data controller responsibilities lie with the platform users. 45dgof8 acts as a coordination tool provider only.
