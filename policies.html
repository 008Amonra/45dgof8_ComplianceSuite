Shop Policies

45DGOF8 is a small creative workshop. Physical products are listed and sold through Etsy so payment, checkout, shipping tools, and buyer protections stay on the platform designed for that job.

Where Orders Happen

Orders are placed through the official Etsy shop: etsy.com/shop/45dgof8. This website does not process payments or collect shipping addresses.

Payment

Payment is handled by Etsy checkout. 45DGOF8 does not receive card details through this website.

Shipping

Shipping options, delivery estimates, and shipping prices are shown on Etsy listings before checkout. If a listing is unavailable or shipping is not shown for your region, use Etsy or email to ask before ordering.

Returns and Problems

Returns, damaged item reports, and order problems are handled according to the active Etsy listing and Etsy buyer protection rules. If something arrives wrong, document it with photos and contact through Etsy first.

Privacy

This static website does not run its own checkout, customer account system, or shipping form. Etsy may process order and account data according to Etsy's own policies.

Contact

For product questions, use Etsy messages when possible. You can also contact 45DGOF8 at 45dgof8@gmail.com.
